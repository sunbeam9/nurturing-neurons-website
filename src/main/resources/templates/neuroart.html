Visual Arts Contest

NeuroART

NeuroART is an opportunity for elementary, middle, and high school students to explore the fascinating field of neuroscience. Students are encouraged to blend scientific knowledge of neuroscience with artistic talent to create a visual arts piece inspired by aspects of the nervous system. Students may use their preferred visual arts medium. All submissions should be titled and may be accompanied by an artistic statement by the student (100 words max) that describes how their work relates to neuroscience.

We invite students to explore the complexities of the nervous system and display their artistic skills while supporting the Nurturing Neurons Guild and the Neuromuscular Program at Seattle Children's Hospital.

The deadline to submit entries is March 14th, 2022.
